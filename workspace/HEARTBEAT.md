## Autonomy Configuration

### Cron Jobs (CST Times)
| Job | Schedule | Purpose |
|-----|----------|---------|
| morning-brief | 0 14 * * 1-5 | Daily morning brief (8AM CST) |
| health-check | */30 12-5 * * * | System health every 30min (6AM-11PM CST) |
| weekly-review | 0 0 * * 1 | Weekly summary (Sunday 6PM CST) |

### Timezone Handling
All cron expressions are in UTC. CST = UTC-6 (or UTC-5 during DST).
- 8AM CST = 14:00 UTC
- 6AM CST = 12:00 UTC
- 11PM CST = 05:00 UTC next day
- Sunday 6PM CST = 00:00 UTC Monday

### Channel Routing
